More About The Anime

The Infinity Castle Arc in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba brings together the entire Demon Slayer Corps and the strongest demons in a final, climactic confrontation within a shifting, dimensional fortress.Here is a brief description of the key people and demons involved:Demon Slayers & Key AlliesTanjiro Kamado: The main protagonist, wielding Sun Breathing and Water Breathing. He enters the castle with the goal of killing Muzan and protecting his sister, aiming to master the "Transparent World" to combat Upper Ranks.Nezuko Kamado: Tanjiro’s demon sister, now immune to sunlight. While she is not on the front lines, she takes a drug to become human and is placed under protection to recover her humanity.Zenitsu Agatsuma: A Thunder Breathing user who sheds his cowardice to duel his former senior, Kaigaku, avenging his master with a new technique, the seventh form.Inosuke Hashibira: Feral Beast Breathing user who fights with dual serrated swords. He is driven by vengeance for his mother, Kotoha, and fights Upper Rank Two, Doma.Giyu Tomioka (Water Hashira): A calm, focused swordsman who partners with Tanjiro to face Upper Rank Three, Akaza, unlocking his demon slayer mark.Shinobu Kocho (Insect Hashira): A medic and poison expert who faces Upper Rank Two, Doma, in a deeply personal fight to avenge her sister, Kanae.Kanao Tsuyuri: Shinobu’s tsuguko (apprentice) who battles Doma after arriving to help her sister.Sanemi Shinazugawa (Wind Hashira): Extremely aggressive Hashira who confronts Upper Rank One, Kokushibo, utilizing his rare Marechi blood to disorient demons.Genya Shinazugawa (Demon Slayer): Sanemi’s younger brother who gains demonic powers by eating demon flesh. He plays a crucial role in fighting Kokushibo, even after suffering severe wounds.Gyomei Himejima (Stone Hashira): The strongest Hashira in the corps, he faces Kokushibo with superior strength and endurance.Muichiro Tokito (Mist Hashira): A prodigy who battles Upper Rank One, Kokushibo.Mitsuri Kanroji (Love Hashira): A physically strong Hashira who faces the shifting, complex environment of the castle.Obanai Iguro (Serpent Hashira): A calculating Hashira who supports his allies while managing his own fight against the castle's traps.Tamayo: A demon who joins forces with the slayers, she uses her Blood Demon Art and medical skills to drug Muzan, aiming to turn him human.Yushiro: Tamayo's assistant who uses his Blood Demon Art to assist the Ubuyashikis in mapping the complex Infinity Castle.Kiriya, Kuina, and Kanata Ubuyashiki: The surviving children of Kagaya Ubuyashiki who take over command, mapping the castle and directing the Hashira using Kasugai Crows.Demons & Upper RanksMuzan Kibutsuji: The progenitor of demons, who traps the Demon Slayers in his stronghold to eliminate them all at once.Kokushibo (Upper Rank One): A demon with six eyes and samurai clothing, he is a master of Moon Breathing and confronts the strongest Hashira.Doma (Upper Rank Two): A deceptive demon who leads a cult and fights using ice-based techniques, killer of Shinobu's sister, Kanae.Akaza (Upper Rank Three): A martial arts expert who fights Giyu and Tanjiro, forcing them to exceed their limits before experiencing a redemption of his human past.Nakime (Upper Rank Four): The user of the Biwa, who controls the shifting layout of the Infinity Castle and acts as Muzan's personal scout.Kaigaku (Upper Rank Six): A former Thunder Breathing user and Zenitsu’s senior, who was turned into a demon by Kokushibo.
