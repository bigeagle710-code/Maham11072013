Kamietsu No Yaibi

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a popular anime and manga series set in Taishō-era Japan that follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who makes a living selling charcoal. His life is shattered when he returns home to find his family slaughtered by a demon. The sole survivor is his younger sister, Nezuko, who has been transformed into a demon herself, yet uniquely retains human emotions and cognitive abilities. Vowing to avenge his family and cure his sister, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, a secret organization that uses specialized breathing techniques and Nichirin swords to fight demons, which are former humans possessing supernatural powers and a craving for human flesh.
